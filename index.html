Experience Highlight: Transforming Healthcare Operations

Serving as a volunteer Senior IT Manager and Data Scientist at Kingdom Hospital, Webuye (Jan 2023 - Present), has been a profoundly rewarding experience, allowing me to apply technology directly to enhance patient outcomes and operational effectiveness. In collaboration with a dedicated team of IT interns whom I mentored, we prioritized maintaining high standards of network security, ensuring consistent system uptime for critical hospital functions, and preserving the integrity of vital patient data.

A cornerstone achievement was the development and deployment of predictive analytics models tailored for optimizing patient flow. Through meticulous analysis of historical data and identification of operational bottlenecks, these models led to a remarkable 35% reduction in average patient waiting times. Concurrently, we automated essential hospital management tasks by integrating Frappe-based ERP solutions, substantially improving the accuracy of record-keeping and billing processes, thus fostering a more efficient administrative environment. My responsibilities also extended to enforcing rigorous cybersecurity protocols and implementing robust disaster recovery plans to safeguard sensitive patient information against potential threats.
